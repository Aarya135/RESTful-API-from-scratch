You have reached the front page of the Wiki API

To make get requests:

  • Add "/articles" to the URL to get all articles.
  • Add "/articles/{article title}" to get a specific article.
  • I have provided 5 articles. Their titles are "REST", "API", "Bootstrap", "DOM" and "Jack Bauer".
  • Remember that the titles are case sensitive.

To make POST, PUT, PATCH and DELETE requests, you will require Postman App.
